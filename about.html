About Mr. Pool Leak Repair

13+ Years of Excellence in Pool Leak Detection & Repair

Our Story

Mr. Pool Leak Repair was founded on a simple principle: pool owners deserve honest, expert service backed by real warranties and real results.

For over 13 years, we've been the trusted choice for pool leak detection and repair across the DFW Metroplex. What started as a commitment to excellence has grown into a family-owned business with 550+ five-star reviews and countless satisfied customers.

We don't just fix leaks—we build relationships. Our team, including Azalie and Laura, are known for their exceptional service and personal attention to every customer. When clients remember your staff by name, that's when you know you're doing something right.

Today, we continue to set the standard for pool repair in Texas with industry-leading warranties, expert technicians, and a commitment to customer satisfaction that goes beyond the job.
